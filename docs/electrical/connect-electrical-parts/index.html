Electrical Assembly Step 1: Wiring

Step 1: Wiring
Look at the bottom of the circuit board, under the blue terminal blocks.

One side is labeled for the batteries, the other for the motor. Also there are markings above for red and below for black wires.

The batteries are connected as labeled, but there is one trick for connecting the motor, explained next.
Before wiring the motor, look at your motor mount.

This picture shows the ‘normal’ way to build the mount, after the motor is installed. (Don’t install the motor yet, just compare your mount to the picture.)
This picture shows the ‘flipped’ way to build the motor mount. Either will work, but if you built a ‘flipped’ motor mount, you’ll need to connect the motor wires backwards; red to black and black to red. Only flip the motor wires, not the battery wires.
Now put the wires in the terminal blocks and screw them in.

Once this is done, wait for your glue to dry and put everything together.
